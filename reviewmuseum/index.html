Regardless of the genre, films that achieved ‘fresh’ in audience scores and ‘certified fresh’ on Tomatometer which is based on Rotten Tomatoes.

‘Fresh’ in audience score:
At least 60% of reviews for a film are positive

‘Certified fresh’ in Tomatometer:
Based on the opinions of professional film critics
At least five reviews from Top Critics
Have a minimum of 80 reviews
A consistent Tomatometer score of 75% or higher

There are only professionals' reviews.
